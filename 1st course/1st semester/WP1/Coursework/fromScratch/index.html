Welcome to the In-Between

Places that exist between beginning and end. Transitional spaces, abandoned and forgotten, that evoke a strange sense of nostalgia and unease.

What are Liminal Spaces?

Definition

Liminal spaces are places of transition - hallways, empty parking lots, abandoned overpasses. They are "in-between" places where we don't linger, only pass through.

Psychology

These spaces create a sense of déjà vu and nostalgia. They are simultaneously familiar and alien, real and dreamlike.

The Aesthetic

Characterized by low-quality flash photography, fluorescent lighting, and a lack of human presence. It captures the "uncanny" nature of reality.

Kenopsia

The eerie, forlorn atmosphere of a place that is usually bustling with people but is now abandoned and quiet.
